# Stage 1 — Intelligence Core

Version 7 begins the first production-oriented build stage around three shared systems.

## 1. ETF Universe Foundation

- Provider-agnostic ETF master interface
- Search and filtering service
- Critical-field completeness checks
- Duplicate ticker detection
- Verified/provider/modeled/missing confidence labels
- Seed records remain demonstrations and are not represented as current market facts

## 2. Income Digital Twin v2

- Portfolio cloning through immutable plans
- Base, recession, high-inflation, bull, and custom regimes
- First-year shock modeling
- Monthly contributions and one-time capital
- Reinvestment and withdrawals
- Inflation-adjusted portfolio values
- Income coverage and sustainability status
- Scenario comparison contract

## 3. Income Copilot Runtime

- Question intent routing
- Portfolio, score, Digital Twin, ETF research, and calendar tool routes
- Evidence cards and confidence levels
- Explicit verified-data requirement for live ETF research
- Action-oriented responses

## 4. Income Operating System Score

The existing transparent ten-dimension score is surfaced in the Stage 1 workspace and remains traceable to portfolio inputs. Later Stage 1 increments will replace offline proxies with verified ETF Universe attributes.

## Next Stage 1 increments

1. Persistent scenario repository and scenario comparison workspace
2. Full ETF provider import adapters and normalized distribution tables
3. Copilot conversation context and page-level actions
4. Historical distribution and holdings snapshots
5. Score v2 using verified expenses, liquidity, distribution stability, and tax character
