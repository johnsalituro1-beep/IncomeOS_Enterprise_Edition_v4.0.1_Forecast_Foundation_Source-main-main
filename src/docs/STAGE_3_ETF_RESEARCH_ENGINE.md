# Stage 3 — ETF Research Engine

Version 9 introduces the second-generation ETF research layer.

## Delivered

- Searchable ETF Research Terminal connected to local portfolio holdings.
- Structured ETF research profiles with strategy, role, strengths, considerations, and risk classification.
- Distribution intelligence with indicated yield, modeled weekly distribution, cadence, and sustainability review flags.
- Portfolio-fit analytics measuring capital weight, annual income contribution, concentration, and strategy role.
- Explainable six-dimension scorecard.
- Evidence ledger marking every research field as verified, provider supplied, modeled, or missing.
- Modeled peer relationships compatible with the future ETF Universe knowledge graph.
- Dedicated Stage 3 control room and build-readiness status.

## Data policy

All current prices, distribution figures, categories, and frequencies originate from the offline demonstration portfolio. They are explicitly labeled as modeled. Issuer methodology, holdings, benchmark, expense ratio, AUM, tax character, and historical return data remain unavailable until a verified provider is connected.
