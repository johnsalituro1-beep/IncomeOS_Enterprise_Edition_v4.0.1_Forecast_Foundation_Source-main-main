# Version 8 — Stage 2 Portfolio Intelligence

## Delivered

- Portfolio Intelligence Engine v2 with eight explainable dimensions.
- Income Operating System Score v2 with ten weighted components.
- Concentration, strategy, yield, and risk alerts.
- Driver-level explanations and recommended next actions.
- Stage 2 control-room page and navigation route.
- Explicit modeled-data confidence labels.

## Intelligence dimensions

1. Income Stability
2. Distribution Quality
3. Yield Quality
4. Diversification
5. Cash-Flow Consistency
6. Drawdown Risk
7. Growth Potential
8. Portfolio Resilience

## Current data limitation

The release uses portfolio values entered in the application and category-based offline proxies. It does not claim verified historical distribution quality, drawdown, correlation, expense ratio, tax character, or holdings overlap. Those fields will be upgraded when the ETF Universe is connected to verified provider feeds.
