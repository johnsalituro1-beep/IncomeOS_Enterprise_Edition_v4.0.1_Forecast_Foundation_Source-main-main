# Income OS Offline Milestone — Priorities 1–10

This increment integrates ten product workstreams into one offline architecture:

1. Income Mission Control with cash events, goal flight plan, intelligence feed, and Income Copilot.
2. Comparison Studio foundation retained and positioned for multi-ETF analytics.
3. Portfolio Optimization Lab with interactive allocation modeling.
4. Dividend Calendar 2.0 architecture through reusable modeled cash events.
5. Portfolio Flight Recorder 2.0 with manual snapshots and historical timeline.
6. Income Intelligence v3 through explainable mission alerts and copilot evidence.
7. ETF Universe Importer with CSV validation, duplicate detection, and quality scoring.
8. Professional Reporting Suite with portable HTML export.
9. Mobile Companion product-design prototype.
10. Commercial Edition readiness matrix covering accounts, cloud, sharing, subscriptions, advisor workspaces, audit logs, and feature flags.

## Verification limitation
Dependency installation and a production Vite build remain blocked by registry/network access. Modules were developed as deterministic TypeScript/React source and statically reviewed offline.

## Data limitation
All market events, distributions, yields, and demonstration holdings remain modeled placeholders until verified providers are connected.
