# Stage 4 — Mission Control 2.0

Version: 10.0.0-stage-4-mission-control

## Objective

Turn the Income OS home experience into a decision-oriented operating surface rather than a passive dashboard.

## Delivered

- Five-metric command strip covering value, weekly income, cash window, Income OS Score, and goal gap.
- Income Pulse visualization with modeled 12-week distributions and mission-target line.
- Rule-driven Priority Queue for goal gaps, concentration, covered-call exposure, and crypto-income exposure.
- Configurable widget registry with local in-session toggles.
- Integrated cash calendar.
- Digital Twin base-case snapshot.
- Research-watch signals.
- Income Copilot command bar and briefing panel.
- Responsive terminal layouts.

## Data status

All portfolio facts originate from the local modeled portfolio. Live prices, announced distributions, verified holdings, news, and market events require a connected provider and are intentionally not represented as verified.

## Next build dependency

Stage 5 should deepen the Strategy Builder and connect recommendations to Digital Twin scenarios, the Income OS Score, and the ETF Universe evidence layer.
