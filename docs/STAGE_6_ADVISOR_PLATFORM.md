# Version 12 — Stage 6 Advisor Platform

This release adds a functional offline advisor-platform foundation: client, household, portfolio, role, task, notes, CRM and reporting contracts; advisor aggregation calculations; and a routed Advisor Platform control room.

## Scope delivered
- Multi-client and multi-account domain
- Household aggregation engine
- Advisor dashboard metrics
- Client-review and task views
- Role and permission vocabulary
- CRM notes and goal models
- White-label/reporting architecture

All data is demonstration data. Production deployment requires authentication, encrypted persistence, tenant isolation, audit logging, and compliance review.
