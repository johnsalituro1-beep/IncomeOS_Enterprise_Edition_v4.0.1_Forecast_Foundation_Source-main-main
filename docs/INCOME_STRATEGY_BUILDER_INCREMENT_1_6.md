# Income Strategy Builder™ — Offline Development Increment 1–6

## Implemented

1. Guided Strategy Builder UI: goals, assumptions, account preferences, risk tolerance and distribution preference.
2. Explainable Optimization Engine v1: transparent weighted scoring, deterministic allocation generation and ranked outcomes.
3. Strategy Templates: Income + Growth, Conservative Income, Income Maximizer, Retirement Income, Dividend Growth and Weekly Cash Flow.
4. Scenario Comparison Studio: three-way comparisons for income, modeled yield, growth, constraints and horizon value.
5. Strategy Report Generator: portable JSON export with assumptions, constraints, complete allocations, scores, projections and disclosures.
6. Goal Alignment Engine: explicit constraint checks, goal progress, risk-fit scoring and plain-language observations.

## Data status

The candidate universe in this increment is deliberately modeled and clearly marked. It must not be presented as verified ETF data. The ETF Knowledge Base provider pipeline will replace the placeholders after live-source validation.

## Verification status

Source architecture and route wiring were completed offline. Dependency installation, TypeScript compilation and the Vite production build remain pending registry access.
